# AI Industry Report 2025

## Executive Summary

Artificial Intelligence adoption increased significantly in 2025 across healthcare, finance, and education sectors.

OpenAI reported strong enterprise adoption of GPT-based solutions, while Google expanded Gemini integrations into productivity tools.

## Market Statistics

- Global AI market size reached $391 billion in 2025.
- Enterprise AI spending increased by 37%.
- AI-assisted customer support reduced operational costs by 28%.
