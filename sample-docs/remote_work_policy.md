# Remote Work Policy

## Work Hours

Employees are expected to work between 9 AM and 5 PM local time.

## Equipment

The company provides:
- laptops
- monitors
- VPN access

## Security Requirements

Employees must:
- use MFA authentication
- avoid public Wi-Fi
- encrypt sensitive documents

## Leave Policy

Employees receive:
- 20 annual leave days
- 10 sick leave days
- public holidays

## Communication Tools

Approved tools:
- Slack
- Zoom
- Notion
- Jira
